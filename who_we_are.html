History

Both being already in love with food -and mainly desserts- after they met eatch other their shared passion had to become something more! So there was the ‘birth’ of their lovechild named GONUTS.

Quality Ingredients

Our toppings, glaze and dough are made fresh with local ingredients. We believe in ‘the closer you buy it, the fresher it is’. Come pay us a visit and taste for yourself!

Made Fresh Everyday

As we work with local ingredients, it is more than logical that every part of the donuts are made fresh on a daily basis.

Ananda

Has always had a passion for food since he was very young. When he was just five his Mother couldn’t keep him out of the kitchen! At 18 he started university to become a lawyer but realised that he already lost his soul to food. After quitting school he met Fien and they didn’t only share their love for each other but also their love for food.

Fien

Spend most of her youth in the candyshop around the corner as she is the biggest sweet tooth in the world. After middle school she started working as a confectioner in the local bakery. One sunny morning in march, a friendly guy walked in and ordered two donuts, one for himself and one for her. And so the story of GONUTS began.
